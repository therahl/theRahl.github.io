About Me
I am a web developer in San Diego, California. Here you will find some examples of my work, contact information, and blog posts.

Quick Facts
You can regularly find me hiking at Torrey Pines or hitting the surf at Scripps Pier. I used to be a chef, but now enjoy cooking at home for friends and family. You can also find me in one of the Free Code Camp gitter channels helping students learn fullstack javascript. On my blog, I like to write about new (to me) technologies and journal my current interests and projects.

Why Code?
My path to coding began as a hobby in junior high school where I learned html and css. My friends and I felt empowered by the internet and our ability to create something accessible to the whole world. Lead by this sense of empowerment, we did what any nerdy teenagers would do - we built a gaming website and my interest in web development grew from there.

Skills
