About Me
I am a fullstack web developer in San Diego, California. I am passionate about building beautiful user interfaces and full featured web applications. I enjoy working on the front end and back end and am always looking for opportunities to expand my skillset as a developer.

Quick Facts
You can regularly find me hiking at Torrey Pines or hitting the surf at Scripps Pier. I used to be a chef, but now enjoy cooking at home for friends and family. You can also find me in one of the Free Code Camp gitter channels helping students learn fullstack javascript the same way I did.

Why Code?
My path to coding began as a hobby in junior high school where I learned html and css. My friends and I felt empowered by the internet and our ability to create something accessible to the whole world. Lead by this sense of empowerment, we did what any nerdy teenagers would do - we built a gaming website, and my interest in web development grew from there.
